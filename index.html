🕳️ Hole in the Wall: Shape Squad

Work together to fill the shape with your bodies — before time runs out!

About the camera: It only looks for where bodies are versus an empty-room reference. Nothing is recorded, saved, or sent anywhere — it all stays in this browser tab.
How it plays: There are 3 rounds. Each round rolls 3 random shapes in a row — fully automatic, no tapping needed! Round 1: name is said, guide stays on screen. Round 2: name is said, then the guide flashes during a 3‑2‑1 countdown before vanishing. Round 3: just the name — no guide at all. Then a full review with everyone's scores, plus victory music!
