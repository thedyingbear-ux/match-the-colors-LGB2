🕳️ Hole in the Wall: Shape Squad

Work together to fill the shape with your bodies — before time runs out!

About the camera: It only looks for where bodies are versus an empty-room reference. Nothing is recorded, saved, or sent anywhere — it all stays in this browser tab.
How it plays: There are 2 stages. Stage 1 — Hole in the Wall: the shape's outline stays on screen the whole time while the squad fits themselves into it. Stage 2 — Listening Challenge: no outline at all — just the shape name spoken three times, then a 3‑2‑1 countdown, and the squad has to remember the shape from memory! Pick which shapes to use below to match today's lesson. Fully automatic — no tapping needed once it starts.
